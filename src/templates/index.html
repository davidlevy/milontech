Why do we need this glossary?

The Israeli high-tech scene is a unique ecosystem fueled by its own language: Heblish. It's a beautiful mix of English software engineering jargon seamlessly woven into everyday Hebrew grammar. Whether you're an Oleh Chadash, a junior dev fresh out of bootcamp, or an international partner trying to decode your Israeli team's Slack messages—this kit is your survival guide to speaking fluent Startup Nation.
